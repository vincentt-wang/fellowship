The Mission

Backed before the world catches on.

By the time a founder is fundable, everyone wants in. The Fellowship is for the stage just before that, when the work is real but the market has not noticed yet. We show up first, with space, mentors and a stage at Demo Day.

Talent, not credentials

We back the person already building, regardless of school, title or network.

In early

We get in when conviction is scarce and worth the most, long before the round is oversubscribed.

Momentum over curriculum

No fixed syllabus. Four weeks built to move your one real project forward, fast.

In the lineage of the invisible college. Community first, backed early, chosen on what you build.

How it works · 4 weeks

A mentor a week. One goal at a time.

No lecture hall and no fixed syllabus. Every Saturday runs on three things: a flight mentor's real story, an honest founder update, and one goal for the week ahead.

Week 1Aug 15 · Sat Frame the problem

Kick off with a flight mentor. Cut the project down to the one job your first users hire it for, and commit to a goal for the week.

Week 2Aug 22 · Sat Build the evidence

Report last week in the open. A new flight mentor pressure-tests demand and feasibility, then you lock the next goal.

Week 3Aug 29 · Sat Sharpen the story

Turn real results into a narrative an investor believes. A mentor stress-tests the pitch and the numbers.

Week 4Sep 5 · Sat Dress rehearsal

Full run-through in front of the final-week judge, so every project gets an honest read before the stage.

Demo DaySep 9 · Wed BobaTech Summit stage

Selection first: the host and judges pick 3 to 5 teams. Then you pitch at the BobaTech Summit, Asia's largest Gen Z founder stage.
